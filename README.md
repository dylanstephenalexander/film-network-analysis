## Social Networks and Success: Analyzing Collaboration Patterns in Major Films
### Data:
Our data is from Kaggle, including the [IMDb Actors and Movies](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rishabjadhav/imdb-actors-and-movies/data?) and [TMDB Movies](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/alanvourch/tmdb-movies-daily-updates) datasets. The latter is used for our income-based research questions.
